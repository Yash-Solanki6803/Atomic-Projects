About Manthan

MANTHAN is a flagship technical event of Department of Computer Science, Ganpat University since 2010. Students from reputed institutes and universities from India participate and show case their technical abilities and innovations during this tech fest. The key attractions of the tech fest are the events related to cyber security domain. Events such as Capture the Flag, Web Pen testing, Cyber Crime Scene and Game of Encryption will not only build confidence but also aware the students and audience who want to make their career in the field of Cyber Security which is at present one of the emerging area to focus in the country like INDIA. Events like Web Diseno will surely bring out the creative minds from the country.

The participants will get a valuable opportunity to present innovative ideas, to interact and exchange their thoughts with fellow participants of various Institutes/Universities of India and they will surely carry with them memories to cherish forever.

Online CTF

“Come ...Hack…Conquer”

The Cyber Security competition CTF (Capture the Flag), is designed for challenging participants to solve or capture computer security problems and defend computer systems. The mixed Jeopardy, Attack-Defense are the basic types.

Rules of CTF

  • Denial of service is strictly prohibited
  • Brute-forcing the Flag is not allowed
  • Clues will be given in direct/indirect form in our discord App /Manthan-2020 channel
  • Duration: 24 hrs.
  • Registration Fees: 200/- per participant
  • Prize: 15000/-

*Important note : CTF will be played individual only no teams allowed

Web Pentesting

“Hack and become an Admin”

The main objective of penetration testing is to identify security weaknesses in web application. In today’s emerging internet era information security is core requirement. Web Pentesting competition encourages participants to dig-out security weaknesses of Web Applications.

Rules of Web Pentesting:

  • Team Size: Up to 3 Participants
  • Denial of service is strictly prohibited
  • Brute-forcing is not allowed
  • The guide-lines of the tools will be provided during the event
  • Duration: 3 hrs
  • Registration Fees: 200/- per participant
  • Prize: 5000/-

*The prize will be shared among the winners.

Poster Presentation

The poster presentation is a way to communicate the research or understanding of a topic in a short and concise format which includes two elements, the first is a poster and the second is a brief explanation of the same. The limitation of the text is about one-fourth of the poster space, and use "visuals" (Graphs, Photographs, Schematics, Maps etc.) to present your ideas.

Rules of Poster presentation

  • Participants have to make poster in advance
  • Topic: Computer Science and Technology based on Research and Innovation
  • Team Size: Up to 2 Participants
  • Poster Size: Must be mounted on a 1mtr x 1mtr board paper
  • Banner displaying: Your name, Department (Affiliated University) & title of poster should be mentioned at top-center of the poster.
  • Registration Fees: 200/- per participant
  • Prize: 1st, 2nd, 3rd Trophy
